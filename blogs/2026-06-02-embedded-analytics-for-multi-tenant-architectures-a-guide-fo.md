Most SaaS products use multi-tenant architecture, yet most embedded analytics tools are not designed for it, creating a gap where implementation projects stall and security risks emerge. This article emphasizes that implementing embedded analytics in multi-tenant environments requires particular attention to tenant isolation, authentication, security, and scalability to avoid engineering complexity and security exposure. Sisense provides guidance for product teams navigating these architectural challenges in their SaaS platforms.
