Embedded analytics for SaaS has evolved from a nice-to-have feature into a core driver of product differentiation, customer retention, and expansion revenue. Today's users expect insights directly within the applications they already use rather than through disconnected BI tools or static reports. SaaS companies delivering intuitive, in-context analytics create more engaging experiences, enable faster decision-making, and increase platform value overall.
