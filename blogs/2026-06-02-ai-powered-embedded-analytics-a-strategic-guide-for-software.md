Data has never been more available, but availability was never the hard part — understanding it and acting on it fast enough to matter is where most products still fall short. This article introduces AI-powered embedded analytics as a solution enabling organizations to deliver intelligent insights directly within software applications, eliminating the need for users to access separate business intelligence tools. Sisense Team provides a strategic guide for software creators looking to embed AI-driven analytics capabilities into their products.
